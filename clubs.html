Math Team

The UCLS Math Team competes in different math competitions every year. In particular, Ashwin has competed in many NSML (North Suburban Math League) meets that occured once to twice a month; he also competes in the annual ICTM (The Illinois Council of Teachers of Mathematics) contents and has gone to the State Competition in Urbana Champagne, Illinois all four years of High School.
Math has always been Ashwin's favorite subject in High School and Math team has furthered his knowledge in it.
