Clubs

Math Team (3 Years Varsity)

The UCLS Math Team competes in different math competitions every year. In particular, Ashwin has competed in many NSML (North Suburban Math League) meets that occured once to twice a month; he also competes in the annual ICTM (The Illinois Council of Teachers of Mathematics) contents and has gone to the State Competition in Urbana Champaign, Illinois all four years of High School.

Math has always been Ashwin's favorite subject in High School and Math team has furthered his knowledge in it.
Science Team (3 Years Varsity)

The UCLS Science Team attends a couple of competitions anually: WYSE (Worldwide Youth in Science and Engineering), ISO (Illinois Science Olympiad), and TEAMS (Tests of Engineering Aptitude, Mathematics, and Science). Ashwin is an avid member of all three of these varsity teams at the Lab Schools. In the WYSE competition, Ashwin competes in the Computer Science and Engineering Graphics tests; Ashwin builds the Electric Vehicle using circuitry and coding to assemble a car; Ashwin competed in the 2016 National TEAMS competition in Nashville, Tennesse and achieved 5th place in the nation for the 8-person competition in which Ashwin was a part of.


Code at Lab (1 Year) Code@Lab is a new club at lab that has several coding competitions that are attended every year.
